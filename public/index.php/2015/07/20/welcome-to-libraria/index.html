Welcome to LIBRARIA

July 20, 2015 · acorsin

LIBRARIA is a collective of anthropology, archaeology and social studies of science journals and learned societies that have teamed with the Public Knowledge Project and the Scholarly Publishing and Academic Resources Coalition (SPARC), two of world’s leading experts in open access scholarly communications, to research cooperative alternatives to the existing ecology of academic publishing.

You can read more about LIBRARIA, including a list of our current members, here. You can find out more about our aims and objectives here, and about the Open Access Cooperative Publishing Study of which we are a part here.
